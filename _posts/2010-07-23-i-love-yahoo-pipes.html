I wanted to add a section to this site, called Echoes, that would just "paste" my recent (anti)social activity, such as tweets, bookmarks etc. The idea was:

  1. grab the content from social networks,
  2. aggregate, transform and filter it to contain only recent activity,
  3. output the desired result as JSON (so I can include it easily into content on my site with a bit of javascript).

And that's just what Yahoo Pipes does. I couldn't stop playing with it. The result is the echoes page here.

Echoes pipe is public, so feel free to view the source or clone it. You can also view my jQuery code for including the JSON pipe output in my markup.
